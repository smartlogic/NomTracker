What is nomtracker?

Nomtracker is a simple tool that allows you to keep track of money you owe to people and money owed to you by others.

If you regularly hang out with the same group of people and find it a hassle to keep track of who owes whom money, then nomtracker is the tool for you.

Read more about how nomtracker came about.

How do I get started?

Sign Up for an account and then start creating credits and debts.

When you create a credit or debt the other person will receive an email stating as much. Then both of you can login to nomtracker to track your respective credits and debts.
